INFO



Appointments

For appointments please stop by our shop from Tuesday to Saturday between 11-12 pm. In this period we will be ready to make appointments and give you the right guidance. Our experience tells us that this is done best face-to-face.If you’re not able to show up during these hours, you can send us an email or give us a call.Please respect that we are fully concentrated with our customers after 12.00.When making an appointment you must pay a non-refundable deposit of DKK 1.000. The deposit is deducted from the final price.If you’re not showing up without letting us know, you’ll lose your deposit.



Shop Policies

Don’t bother if you are:

  • Under the age of 18
  • Under the influence of alcohol or drugs

When getting your tattoo:

  • Show up on time!
  • Make sure that you are well rested and that you have eaten a meal before your appointment.
  • Remember to always let us know if you have allergies or other medical conditions.
  • You’re welcome to bring a friend but our working area is for our customers and us only.


Payment

Payment is due at the end of the session. We accept cash and Mobilepay.

Hygiene

Hygiene is highly prioritized at Le Fix Tattoo. In order to create a safe and pleasant experience for our customers, we continuously keep ourselves updated on the latest types of pigment and tattoo equipment.It’s important that you follow the guidelines we give you regarding the aftercare of your new tattoo.
